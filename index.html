Features

Intelligence meets
nutrition tracking

Every feature designed to give you confidence in what you eat — and peace of mind about your health.

AI Food Scanner

Point your camera at any food — a home-cooked meal, restaurant dish, or packaged snack. Our AI identifies what you're eating, estimates portions, and returns a complete nutritional breakdown in seconds.

Safety Alerts

Instant warnings for foods that may spike glucose or pose risks for your specific diabetes type. Color-coded: safe, caution, dangerous.

Weekly Stats

Tap any bar to see the day's macros.

Auto Meal Sorting

Scanned food is auto-categorized into breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snack based on time of day.

Full Macro Breakdown

Privacy First

End-to-end encrypted storage on Supabase. Food photos are analyzed and discarded — never stored by AI services. We don't sell, share, or monetize your health data. Ever.

Multilingual

Full support for English, Russian, and Uzbek. Interface, AI analysis, and safety alerts — all in your preferred language. Covering 7+ diabetes types with personalized guidance.
