Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI food scanner work?

Simply point your camera at any food or pick a photo from your gallery. Our AI analyzes the image and provides detailed nutritional information including calories, proteins, carbs, fats, sugar, and glycemic index.

Is DiaBuddy free?

DiaBuddy offers a free tier with basic features. Premium features are available through in-app subscription.

How accurate is the nutritional analysis?

Our AI provides estimates based on visual analysis. For precise measurements, we recommend verifying with food packaging labels when available.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Profile, scroll down, and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

What languages are supported?

DiaBuddy currently supports Russian, English, and Uzbek languages.

Is my health data safe?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
